Anime Reviews

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Two brothers try to play God, lose a few limbs, and accidentally invent trauma alchemy. The gold standard of anime storytelling - literally.

Frieren: Beyond Journey’s End

What happens after the hero’s journey? Apparently… a thousand years of emotional damage and elf-sized existential dread.

Steins;Gate

A mad scientist, a microwave, and way too many timelines. The result? Peak “Don’t touch the past” energy with a sprinkle of El Psy Kongroo.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Cursed energy, chaotic sorcerers, and one very overworked teacher. Basically, if exorcisms were a contact sport.

My Hero Academia

A world where 80% have powers, and the other 20% have character development arcs. Crying, friendship, and lots of yelling included.

One Punch Man

A bald guy defeats everyone in one punch… and still can’t defeat boredom. The real villain? The lack of challenge.
