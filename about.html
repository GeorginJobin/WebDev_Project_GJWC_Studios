About

Hola! I’m Sofia Martinez, the owner of Anime Haven - a place where anime, manga and webtoon fans can feel right at home!

I’ve been a passionate anime fan for as long as I can remember. Whether it’s finding the next big series, rewatching old favourites, discovering new releases, anime has always been a huge part of my life. That love is what inspired me to create Anime Haven - a shop dedicated to sharing the fun, creativity, and community that anime brings to all of us. And now, as Anime Haven launches online, I hope to share my passion with the whole world.

At Anime Haven, we believe anime is more than just entertainment - it’s a culture, an art form, and a way to connect with others who share the same enthusiasm. Our goal is to bring that energy online with a bright, welcoming space where fans can explore, discuss, and celebrate anime together.

Whether you’re hunting for collectibles, discovering hidden gems, or joining conversations about your favourite shows, Anime Haven is your spot to connect and be inspired.

So, if you love anime as much as I do - welcome! I’m so excited to share this journey with you.

– Sofia Martinez
Owner & Founder of Anime Haven
