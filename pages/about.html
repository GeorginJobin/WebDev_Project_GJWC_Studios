About

Hi — I’m Sofia Martinez. I own Anime Haven, a place where anime, manga, and webtoon fans can feel right at home.

Our mission is simple: share the best shows, help fans discover new favourites, and build a friendly community.

What you’ll find here

  • Short, clear reviews and recommendations
  • News about new releases and hidden gems
  • A friendly shop for collectibles and merch
  • Conversations and community discussions

A bit about why I started this

I’ve loved anime for as long as I can remember. Creating Anime Haven lets me share that passion — the fun, the creativity, and the people who love it.

Whether you’re hunting for collectibles, discovering hidden gems, or joining conversations about your favourite shows, Anime Haven is a calm place to connect and be inspired.

– Sofia Martinez
Owner & Founder of Anime Haven
